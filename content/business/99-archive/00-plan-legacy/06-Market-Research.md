## The Brutal Truth

### Stats That Humble

- 97% of YouTubers do not make a living from YouTube
- Most newsletters have 0 paid subscribers
- Average time from start to sustainable income: 18-36 months
- The creator economy has a massive survivorship bias (you only hear about winners)

### Why You Do It Anyway

1. **You are already doing this for yourself.** The 300 files exist. Writing about it is incremental effort.
2. **Dafnck Studio pays the bills.** No pressure to monetize immediately.
3. **The philosophy is real.** It is not manufactured for content. It comes from lived experience.
4. **The worst case is still good.** Even if zero people pay, you have a portfolio of writing, a YouTube channel, and a Twitter presence. All useful for other goals.
5. **The message matters.** Millions of people are anxious about AI. Offering a philosophical anchor is valuable regardless of revenue.
