## Why 50 EUR and Not 8 EUR

| 8 EUR/month | 50 EUR/month |
|-------------|-------------|
| Need 10,417 subs for 1M/year | Need 1,667 subs for 1M/year |
| Commodity pricing ("just another newsletter") | Premium positioning ("this is different") |
| Attracts casual readers | Attracts serious thinkers |
| Low commitment = low engagement | High commitment = high engagement |
| Race to the bottom | Stands alone in the market |

## Honest Reality Check

### What Could Go Wrong

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| ADHD inconsistency | High | Kills growth | Batch creation, templates, accountability |
| Nobody pays 50 EUR | Medium | No revenue | Start at 30 EUR, prove value, raise |
| Low traction first 6 months | High | Discouragement | 12-month commitment before evaluating |
| Content feels forced | Medium | Audience feels it | Only write what is genuine |
| Burnout | Medium | Everything stops | 25h/week cap, batch creation |

### What the Stats Say

- 97% of YouTubers do not make a living
- Median newsletter income: ~$0 (most have zero paid subs)
- Average time to 1,000 subs: 12-18 months
- 5-10% free-to-paid conversion rate (good newsletters)

### Why You Can Beat the Stats

1. **You have something to say** - Not recycled content, genuine philosophical contribution
2. **You live it** - 300+ files prove this is real
3. **Unique intersection** - Nobody else occupies AI + philosophy + human liberation
4. **Dafnck Studio** - Client work pays bills, no pressure to monetize immediately
5. **INFP superpower** - Writing is natural, not forced
