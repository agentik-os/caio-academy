# Content Strategy - Two Pillars

> "What remains when AI does everything? Everything that makes us human."

---

## Pillar 1: The Inner Game

### What It Is

Raw, philosophical content about being human in the AI era. Not tutorials. Not hacks. Deep reflections on identity, consciousness, freedom, creativity, and what it means to exist when machines can produce.

### Platforms

Twitter (daily) + Substack (weekly essay + paid journal)

### Tone

Intimate. Philosophical. Provocative. No corporate speak. You speaking directly to people who feel lost in the AI revolution.

### Content Types

| Type | Frequency | Platform | Example |
|------|-----------|----------|---------|
| Daily reflection | Daily | Twitter | "Day 47. AI wrote a better email than me today. Instead of feeling replaced, I felt free. That email was never me anyway." |
| Philosophical thread | 2-3x/week | Twitter | "AI is not replacing you. It is revealing who you actually are. (thread)" |
| Weekly essay (free) | 1x/week | Substack | "The Productivity Trap: Why doing more was never the point" |
| Journal entry (paid) | 2-3x/week | Substack | Behind-the-scenes emotional content. Doubts, realizations, raw moments. |
| Framework breakdown | 2x/month | Substack + Twitter | "Human 3.0: What comes after optimizing yourself to death" |

## Pillar 2: Build On My Need

### What It Is

Practical, empowering demonstrations of building your own life system with AI. Not "use this tool." Rather: "here is how I built what I needed, and how you can build what YOU need."

### Platforms

YouTube (weekly) + Substack (building posts)

### Tone

Show don't tell. Empowering. "Watch me build this, then build your own." Not prescriptive.

### Content Types

| Type | Frequency | Platform | Example |
|------|-----------|----------|---------|
| Build video | 1x/week | YouTube | "I built my own health protocol with AI. Here is the process." |
| System tour | 1x/month | YouTube + Substack | "My 300-file life operating system (tour + principles)" |
| Deep dive | 2x/month | YouTube | "How I use Claude as a thinking partner, not a tool" |
| Templates (paid) | Monthly | Substack paid | Prompts, starting points, markdown frameworks |
| Behind-the-scenes | 2x/month | YouTube + Substack | "Real-time: building a meditation tracking system" |

**The Life OS:**

**Health System (Build Your Own):**

**Life Design:**
- From vision to daily protocol (the complete chain)
- The 4-account financial automation system

### YouTube Specifics

**Video Structure:**
- Hook (0-30 sec): Philosophical question or impressive result
- Context (30 sec-2 min): Why this matters for being human
- The Build (5-15 min): Screen recording, real-time process
- Reflection (2-3 min): What this means, what it changed
- CTA (30 sec): Subscribe, newsletter link

**Title Frameworks:**
- "I Built [System] with AI. It Changed How I [Result]."
- "Why [Common Belief About AI] Is Wrong"
- "The [Framework] That Made Me Stop [Old Behavior]"
- "[Number] Things AI Taught Me About Being Human"
- "I Replaced [Apps/Systems] with [Simple Solution]. Here Is How."

**Thumbnail Rules:**
- Your face with genuine emotion
- 3-4 words maximum
- High contrast, readable on mobile
- One clear visual element
- No clickbait. Authenticity IS the click.

## Content Production (ADHD-Optimized)

### Batch Creation Sunday

Do NOT create daily. Batch create on Sunday for the entire week.

| Sunday Block | Time | Output |
|-------------|------|--------|
| 9-10 AM | Planning: topics, hooks, outlines | Week mapped |
| 10-12 PM | Writing: 2 Substack posts + 3 Twitter threads | 5 pieces |
| 12-1 PM | Break | Recovery |
| 1-3 PM | YouTube: script + outline | 1 script ready |
| 3-4 PM | Scheduling: queue all content | Everything scheduled |

Saturday is for filming + editing the YouTube video.

### ADHD Guardrails

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Blank page paralysis | Templates for each content type |
| Perfectionism | 80% rule: publish at 80%, iterate based on feedback |
| Scope creep | Max lengths: Tweet (280), Thread (10 tweets), Essay (2,000 words), Video (15 min) |
| Inconsistency | Batch creation eliminates daily decision-making |
| Energy crashes | Only create during peak energy (morning) |
| Overthinking | Write from feeling first, edit for clarity second |

## Metrics That Matter

### Ignore

- Total followers (means nothing without resonance)
- Total views (means nothing without conversion)
- Likes (easy, meaningless)

### Track Weekly

| Metric | Platform | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| Profile visits | Twitter | Are people curious enough to check you out? |
| Thread saves/bookmarks | Twitter | Do people want to come back to this? |
| Subscriber growth | Substack | Is the audience growing? |
| Free-to-paid conversion | Substack | Does the free content prove enough value? |
| Watch time average | YouTube | Do people stay? |
| Messages/replies | All | Are people talking back? |
| Revenue | Substack | Is this sustainable? |

The only metric that truly matters: **do people come back without being asked?**

## The Content Philosophy

Every piece of content should do ONE of these:

1. **Make someone question an assumption** they did not know they had
2. **Show someone they are not alone** in feeling lost in the AI era
3. **Empower someone to build** their own system instead of buying yours
4. **Reveal something real** about your journey that others hide

If a piece of content does not do at least one of these, do not publish it.
