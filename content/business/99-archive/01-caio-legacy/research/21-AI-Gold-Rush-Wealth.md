|                                                |          | = $25-50M |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------+-----------+

AI VALUATION PREMIUM:
- Firms embedding AI see 40-100% valuation uplift
- Proprietary AI assets create competitive barriers
- Recurring ML model licensing = higher-quality revenue
- Data assets compound over time (flywheel effect)

KEY VALUE DRIVERS FOR SOLO AI COMPANIES:

  1. PROPRIETARY TECHNOLOGY
     Unique algorithms, pre-trained models, custom datasets
     that competitors cannot easily replicate.

  2. RECURRING REVENUE (MRR/ARR)
     Monthly subscriptions > project fees
     Predictable cash flow = higher multiples

  3. DATA MOAT
     More users = more data = better AI = more users
     This flywheel is the ultimate competitive advantage

  4. LOW CHURN
     Net revenue retention > 100% = exponential growth
     Churn < 5% monthly = premium valuation

  5. GROWTH RATE
     > 35% annual growth = premium multiples
     Consistent growth > spiky growth

THE EQUITY ADVANTAGE OF BOOTSTRAPPING:
  VC-funded founders: 10-30% equity at exit
  Bootstrapped + AI: 60-80% equity retained

  Same $5M exit:
  - VC route: You get $500K-$1.5M
