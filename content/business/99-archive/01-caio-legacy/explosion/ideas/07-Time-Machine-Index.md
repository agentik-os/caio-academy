### TTM Score Explained

```
TIME TO MAINSTREAM (TTM) SCORE: 0-100%

0-20%:   NASCENT
         - Mostly academic
         - No commercial products
         - 5-10+ years to mainstream

20-40%:  VERY EARLY
         - Early startups
         - First funding rounds
         - 3-5 years to mainstream

40-60%:  EARLY
         - Growing ecosystem
         - Series A/B companies
         - 2-3 years to mainstream

60-80%:  BREAKING OUT
         - Clear use cases
         - Big tech entering
         - 1-2 years to mainstream

80-100%: MAINSTREAM IMMINENT
         - Consumer products exist
         - Mass adoption beginning
         - <1 year to mainstream

SIGNALS WE TRACK:
+-- Academic papers (ArXiv, Semantic Scholar)
+-- Patent filings (USPTO, EPO)
+-- Job postings (LinkedIn, Indeed)
+-- Funding rounds (Crunchbase)
+-- GitHub activity
+-- Social sentiment
+-- News mentions
+-- Big tech announcements
```

### TTM Algorithm

```
TTM SCORE CALCULATION:

WEIGHTS:
- Academic activity: 15%
- Patent filings: 15%
- Startup funding: 20%
- Big tech activity: 15%
- Hiring trends: 15%
- Social sentiment: 10%
- Product launches: 10%

FORMULA:
TTM = Σ (signal_score × weight × recency_factor)

RECENCY FACTOR:
- Last 30 days: 1.0x
- 30-90 days: 0.7x
- 90-180 days: 0.4x
- 180+ days: 0.2x

CALIBRATION:
- Historical backtesting
- Compare predictions vs actual adoption
- Continuous refinement
```
