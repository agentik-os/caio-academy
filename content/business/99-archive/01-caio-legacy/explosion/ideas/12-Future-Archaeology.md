## Content Strategy

### Research Process

```
METHODOLOGY:

1. SIGNAL COLLECTION
   - Academic papers (ArXiv, SSRN, etc.)
   - Patent filings
   - Startup funding
   - Lab announcements
   - Policy documents

2. ARTIFACT CURATION
   - Most significant signals
   - Cross-reference multiple sources
   - Verify credibility

3. ANALYSIS
   - Implications
   - Timeline estimation
   - Uncertainty acknowledgment
   - Historical parallels

4. PUBLICATION
   - Clear, accessible writing
   - Visual aids
   - Source citations
   - Actionable insights
```
