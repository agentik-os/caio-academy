### Blockchain Verification

```
WHY BLOCKCHAIN:

1. IMMUTABILITY
   - Predictions can't be changed after the fact
   - No "I never said that"

2. TIMESTAMP PROOF
   - Provable that prediction was made at time X
   - No backdating

3. TRANSPARENCY
   - Anyone can verify
   - Builds trust

IMPLEMENTATION:
+-- Hash prediction content
+-- Store hash on Ethereum (or Polygon for cost)
+-- Link hash to prediction in DB
+-- Verification = compare hashes

COST: ~$0.01 per prediction (Polygon)
```
