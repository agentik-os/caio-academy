# Pipeline d'Idees: Produits a Lancer

> Idees concretes basees sur la recherche, priorisees par potentiel viral + faisabilite

---

## Criteres d'Evaluation

Every idea in this pipeline is scored on four dimensions, each rated from 1 to 5:

- **Viral Potential** -- Can it explode on Twitter? Does mystery amplify it? Does it solve a burning problem?
- **Faisabilite** -- Can it be built in 1-4 weeks? Is the stack already mastered (Claude, Convex, Next.js, Telegram)? Is it viable as a solo founder?
- **Revenue Potential** -- Can it reach $10K MRR? Is the monetization model clear? Does it scale?
- **Anonymat Compatible** -- Can it be managed anonymously? No face-to-camera required? Are payments possible without KYC?

## Tier 1: Haute Priorite -- Lancer Maintenant

### Idee #1: Telegram AI Business Agent

**Concept.** A personal AI agent living inside Telegram that handles email triage and responses, calendar management and booking, task tracking and follow-ups, and deep research on any topic.

**Stack.** Claude API + Composio (850+ integrations) + Telegram Bot.

**Pricing.** $9.99/month (personal) or $29/month (business).

**Target.** Solopreneurs, freelancers, small team founders.

| Critere | Score |
|---------|-------|
| Viral Potential | 4/5 -- Telegram = 1B users, AI is hot |
| Faisabilite | 5/5 -- Stack already mastered |
| Revenue Potential | 4/5 -- $10K-50K MRR possible |
| Anonymat | 5/5 -- Perfect for anonymous operation |
| **Total** | **18/20 -- Haute Priorite** |

**Why now:** Telegram has one billion users and charges zero platform fees. 2026 is the year of the AI agent. Claude + Composio is a powerful stack already in hand. Can ship in 1-2 weeks.

**Viral hook:** "Your personal AI assistant. Right inside Telegram. It handles your emails, your calendar, your tasks. You focus on what matters."

## Recommendation: Ordre de Lancement

**Weeks 1-2: Idea #1 (Telegram AI Agent).** Quick win. The stack is mastered. This validates the market immediately.

**Weeks 3-4: Idea #3 (Content Transformer).** If #1 stalls, pivot to #3. If #1 is working, launch #3 in parallel.

**Month 2: Idea #2 (Developer Portfolio).** Takes longer to build, but the market gap is clear.

**Month 3+: Evaluate and adjust** based on traction from the first products.

## Template de Lancement

### Checklist Pre-Lancement

- Pseudonym/brand chosen
- Anonymous Twitter created
- Landing page with waitlist
- Functional MVP
- 5-10 beta testers

### Checklist Lancement

- Twitter thread announcement
- Product Hunt submission (optional)
- Hacker News Show HN
- Reddit relevant subs
- Direct outreach to 50 prospects

### Checklist Post-Lancement

- Collect feedback
- Iterate rapidly
- Measure: signups, activation, revenue
- Decide: scale or pivot
