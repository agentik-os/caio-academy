# Playbook: Comment Etre un Fondateur Anonyme

> Guide pratique pour construire un empire sans jamais montrer son visage

---

## Les 8 Piliers de l'Anonymat Reussi

1. **Let the work speak.** Quality over personality, always.
2. **Build a track record.** Previous successes -- even anonymous ones -- create credibility.
3. **Embrace the mystery.** Make anonymity a deliberate part of the brand.
4. **Protect operational security.** Avoid paper trails like Delaware LLCs and public registries.
5. **Align with community.** Decentralized governance reduces single points of failure.
6. **Voice without face.** "Voice doxing" represents an acceptable middle ground.
7. **Let metrics prove trust.** Revenue, TVL, and sales volumes build confidence without identity.
8. **Forge high-profile collaborations.** Association with known entities validates credibility.

## 1. Creer l'Identite Anonyme

### Choisir un Pseudonyme

| Criteres | Exemples Bons | Exemples Mauvais |
|----------|---------------|------------------|
| Memorable | Pak, XCOPY, Satoshi | John123, CryptoGuy |
| Unique (googleable) | punk6529, DaFuq Boom | "Builder", "Dev" |
| Sans indices personnels | Abstract, conceptual | Real initials, birth date |
| Coherent avec la marque | The Nothing (Pak) | Random words |

### Options de Structure

**Option A: Simple Pseudonym.** A single name (Pak, XCOPY). Works well for art and thought leadership. Risk: difficult to scale into a team.

**Option B: Mystery Brand.** A project or company name (The Nothing, 6529). Works well for products and protocols. Advantage: can be a team, scales naturally.

**Option C: Anonymous Collective.** Multiple pseudonyms (LooksRare: Guts & Zodd). Works well for DeFi and tech products. Advantage: distributes exposure risk.

**Option D: AI/Non-Human.** Pretend to be AI or unidentifiable (Pak was questioned about being human). Works well for conceptual art. Maximum mystery.

### Generating the Pseudonym

**Methods:**

1. Abstract concept tied to your vision (The Nothing = Pak)
2. Cultural reference (Guts & Zodd = Berserk anime)
3. Numeric (punk6529 = CryptoPunk #6529)
4. Fictional proper name (Satoshi Nakamoto)

## 2. Infrastructure Technique pour l'Anonymat

### Email and Communications Setup

**Email:** ProtonMail or Tutanota (encrypted). No phone number linked. Username without personal clues.

**Domain:** Registrar with WHOIS privacy (Namecheap, Cloudflare). Pay with crypto if possible.

**Social Media:** Separate accounts from your real identity. VPN for creation and usage. No cross-posting with personal accounts.

### Paiements et Finance

| Methode | Niveau Anonymat | Notes |
|---------|-----------------|-------|
| Crypto (BTC, ETH) | High | Use separate wallets |
| Stripe | Medium | Requires identity verification at high volumes |
| Gumroad | Medium | Can withdraw to PayPal |
| LemonSqueezy | Medium | MoR (they handle taxes) |
| Wise Business | Low | Requires identity but multi-currency |

**Warning:** "Beyond a certain volume ($100K+), payment processors will require identity verification. Plan ahead."

### Structure Legale

**Avoid: Delaware LLC.** Public records are easily searchable. This is how the BAYC founders were exposed.

**Consider instead:**

- **Wyoming LLC** -- Better privacy than Delaware, no public disclosure of members
- **Nevis LLC (offshore)** -- High privacy, more complex setup
- **DAO Structure** -- Decentralized, multi-sig with known signatories, founder can remain anonymous

## 3. Construire la Confiance Sans Visage

### Les Substituts de l'Identite

| Substitut | Comment l'Obtenir |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Track Record | Previous successful projects (even anonymous ones) |
| Public Metrics | Revenue, users, volumes -- transparently shared |
| Open Source Code | Proves technical competence |
| Collaborations | Association with known entities |
| Testimonials | Clients/users who vouch for you |
| Multi-sig | Known signatories provide a safety net |

### Case Study: GMX

GMX built trust through four mechanisms working together:

1. **Track record** -- Lead dev (@xdev_10) had built XVIX and Gambit previously. Even anonymously, the history proved competence.
2. **Transparent metrics** -- TVL, volumes, and fees were all public with real-time dashboards.
3. **Multi-sig** -- Signatories known to the community. Even with an unknown founder, funds were secure.
4. **Revenue sharing** -- 100% of fees went to holders, creating perfect incentive alignment.

**Result:** $500M+ TVL, $277B volume, $2M/month in revenue.

### Communication Pattern

**Do:** Share insights and value. Respond to technical questions. Be present but mysterious. Let the work speak. Post cryptic, philosophical content (Pak style).

**Avoid:** Revealing personal details. Photos, videos, or public appearances. Cross-referencing with other accounts. Identifiable linguistic patterns. Predictable posting schedules that reveal your timezone.

## 4. Opsec Avance

### Eviter l'Exposition

| Risk | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|
| Photo metadata | Strip EXIF data before upload |
| Linguistic patterns | Vary writing style |
| Timezone | Post at varied hours |
| IP leaks | VPN always, ideally Tor |
| Cross-account activity | Never connect the accounts |
| Payment trails | Crypto, separate wallets |
| LLC public records | Wyoming over Delaware, or DAO |

### Lecons des Echecs

| Case | How They Were Exposed | Lesson |
|-----|----------------------------|-------|
| BAYC Founders | Delaware LLC public records | Avoid US paper trails |
| 0xSifu | On-chain sleuth (zachxbt) | Separate wallets |
| Ian Macalinao | On-chain patterns | Vary behavior |
| moot (4chan) | Media investigation | At scale, investigation is inevitable |

### Separation des Identites

The recommended setup requires strict isolation:

**Real Identity (Gareth):** Personal email, personal Twitter (@GarethSimono), public projects (AgentikOS, Dafnck Studio). Never cross with the phantom identity.

**Phantom Identity (???):** Dedicated ProtonMail, new Twitter account, VPN/Tor for all activity, separate crypto wallet, separate laptop or browser if possible. Never cross with the real identity.

## 5. Marketing Anonyme

### Strategies Qui Marchent

| Strategie | How to Execute |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Mystery = The Brand | Never reveal, let people speculate |
| Quality Speaks | Product so good it sells itself |
| Thought Leadership | Deep Twitter threads, no personal branding |
| Scarcity | Limited access, gamified waitlists |
| Community Building | Discord, Telegram with mods who do the front-facing work |

### Handling PR Without a Face

**Written Only:** Email interviews. Quotes attributed to the pseudonym. This is the safest approach.

**Voice Only (Voice Dox):** Audio podcasts. Twitter Spaces. An acceptable middle ground.

**Avatar Representation:** A mascot or character. An AI avatar. Enables video content without revealing identity.

**Spokesperson:** Someone else speaks for you. Community manager or CMO. You remain in the shadows.

### Build in Shadow (vs Build in Public)

Build in public says: "I'm Gareth, here is my journey." Personality is the brand. Daily updates, progress shares, personal wins and losses.

Build in shadow says: "Here is what we built. Results speak for themselves." Product is the brand. Selective reveals, milestone announcements, product wins and losses only.

## 6. Scenarios de Sortie

### Quand l'Anonymat N'est Plus Tenable

| Scenario | Action Recommandee |
|----------|-------------------|
| $1M+ revenue, processors demand identity | Offshore legal structure or MoR |
| Media investigation | Have a lawyer ready, statement prepared |
| Acquisition offer | Negotiate partial reveal or full sale |
| Legal issues | Lawyer specializing in crypto/anonymous structures |
| Personal choice to reveal | Control the narrative, make it an event |

### Exit Strategies

**Option 1: Stay Anonymous Forever.** Like Satoshi. Disappear at the peak. Never cash out publicly.

**Option 2: Planned Reveal.** Control the narrative. Transform it into a PR event. "The genius behind X revealed."

**Option 3: Sell the Brand.** Anonymity enables clean transfer. Buyer can continue or reveal. Clean exit with no personal drama.

**Option 4: Transition to Team.** Hire public-facing people. Founder stays in the shadows. Common in DeFi protocols.

## 8. Questions Frequentes

### Can you really stay anonymous for a long time?

Yes, if: you do not grow too large (below the investigation threshold), you protect your opsec rigorously, you avoid legal paper trails (US LLCs), and you never cross your identities. Examples: Satoshi (15+ years), Pak, XCOPY, and the GMX team remain anonymous to this day.

### How do you handle payments at scale?

Options include: Merchant of Record services (LemonSqueezy, Paddle) which handle everything; offshore structures (Nevis, Cayman) which are more complex; DAO treasury for decentralized management; and crypto-only, which is limited but anonymous.

### What if someone doxes you?

Have a lawyer ready. Prepare a statement in advance. Then choose: Option A is to deny and continue. Option B is to embrace it and transform the reveal into PR. The brand will survive if the product is good.

### Does anonymity affect fundraising?

Yes and no. Crypto VCs accept anonymous founders (see LooksRare, Alchemix). Traditional VCs generally do not. The alternative: bootstrap with crypto revenue or revenue-fund.
