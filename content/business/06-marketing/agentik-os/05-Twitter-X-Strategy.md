## 4. Engagement Strategy

### Reply Strategy

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **Reply to your own tweets** | Within 30 min of posting, reply with context/bonus |
| **Reply to big accounts** | 3-5 thoughtful replies/day to accounts with 50K+ followers |
| **Reply to peers** | Genuine engagement with builders in the 1K-20K range |
| **Reply to followers** | EVERY reply on your tweets in the first hour |

### Reply Formula

```
[Acknowledge their point] + [Add unique insight] + [Ask follow-up question]
```

**Bad reply:** "Great point!"
**Good reply:** "This is exactly right. I've seen this with Kommu - our enterprise clients specifically asked for AI agent teams, not chatbots. What's the biggest objection you hear from traditional businesses?"

### Quote Tweet Rules

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Add original insight to the QT | Just retweet with "This" |
| Share personal experience related to the QT | Criticize without offering alternative |
| Tag the original author respectfully | Dunk on smaller accounts |
| Use QTs of big accounts to gain visibility | QT just to argue |

### Who to Engage With Daily

| Tier | Accounts | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Tier 1 (100K+)** | @LiamOttley, @_nateB_jones, @levelsio | 1-2 thoughtful replies/day |
| **Tier 2 (20-100K)** | AI builders, SaaS founders | 3-5 replies/day |
| **Tier 3 (1-20K)** | Fellow builders, your niche | 5-10 replies/day |
| **Tier 4 (<1K)** | New followers, aspiring builders | Reply to all who engage with you |

### DM Strategy

| Trigger | DM Template |
|---------|-------------|
| **New follower (1K+)** | "Hey [name], saw your work on [specific thing]. Really respect the [detail]. Let me know if you ever want to chat about [relevant topic]." |
| **Someone shares your thread** | "Appreciate you sharing that. What resonated most?" |
| **Potential collaboration** | "Been following your content on [topic]. I think our audiences would love a Spaces conversation about [specific angle]. Open to it?" |
| **Potential client** | NO selling in DMs. Build relationship first. Offer value. Let them come to you. |

**DM rules:**
- Never pitch in the first message
- Never send copy-paste templates that feel generic
- Always reference something specific about them
- Keep it to 2-3 sentences
- DM-to-call ratio: aim for 1 call per 20 quality DMs

### Optimization Rules

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Engagement rate <2% consistently | Change content mix |
| Thread bookmarks high but likes low | Content is valuable but hook is weak |
| Replies high but follows low | Not enough CTA to follow |
| DMs increasing | Monetization opportunity - create offer |
| Unfollows spike after a post type | Reduce that content type |
