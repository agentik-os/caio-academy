## 1. Daily Twitter Schedule

### Morning (8-10 AM UTC)
**Tweet 1: Main Value Thread (8:00 AM)**
- Format: Thread (3-8 tweets)
- Topics rotation:
  - Monday: Behind-the-scenes (build in public)
  - Tuesday: Educational (how-to, frameworks)
  - Wednesday: Personal story (vulnerability + lesson)
  - Thursday: Hot take (contrarian but data-backed)
  - Friday: Weekly wins + lessons

**Engagement Window (8:00-8:30 AM)**
- Reply to ALL comments within 15 min
- Reply to 10 mid-size accounts (5K-100K followers)
- Quote tweet 1-2 relevant posts

### Afternoon (2-3 PM UTC)
**Tweet 2: Insight/Question**
- Format: Single tweet with open-ended question
- Purpose: Drive replies (13.5x algo boost)
- Examples:
  - "What's stopping you from starting your first AI agent project?"
  - "Hot take: Most developers are optimizing the wrong metrics. Agree?"

### Evening (7-8 PM UTC)
**Tweet 3: Engagement/Community**
- Format: Poll, question, or retweet with commentary
- Purpose: Stay top-of-mind, build community

## 2. Weekly Content Calendar

### Monday: Build in Public
- Share what you're working on
- Show progress (screenshots, metrics)
- Invite feedback

### Tuesday: Educational Deep Dive
- Full thread (8-12 tweets)
- How-to or framework
- Based on your tools (FORGE, MANIAC, etc.)

### Wednesday: Personal Transformation
- Your journey thread
- Vulnerability + learning
- Resonates with "Alex l'Aspirant" persona

### Thursday: Contrarian Take
- Challenge industry assumptions
- Data-backed or experience-backed
- Sparks debate = high engagement

### Friday: Weekly Reflection
- What you shipped this week
- Lessons learned
- Open question to community

### Saturday: Community Spotlight
- Highlight someone using your tools
- Retweet + add value
- Lower posting volume

### Sunday: Content Batching
- No posting (rest day)
- Batch create next week's content

## 4. Engagement Strategy

### Reply Targets (20-30 replies/day)
**Accounts to engage with:**
- Indie hackers (10-50K followers)
- Claude Code users
- AI/ML content creators
- Developer productivity accounts
- "Building in public" accounts

**Reply quality:**
- Add value, not just "great post!"
- Share your experience
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Quote with added insight

### First 30 Minutes Protocol
**After posting:**
1. Immediately reply to your own thread (continue conversation)
2. Monitor for ANY replies → respond within 5 min
3. Share in relevant communities/DMs
4. Self-engagement triggers algo boost
